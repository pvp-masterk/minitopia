Our Story

MiniTopia was founded by three friends who shared a passion for the chaos and creativity of 6b6t. What started as a small group of players has grown into a respected clan known for both our builds and our ability to survive in the harsh environment of the anarchy server.

We don't limit ourselves to just one aspect of the game. Whether it's constructing impressive bases, exploring the vast world, engaging in PvP, or griefing enemy structures, we do it all with style and coordination.
